# Cryptocurrency-Market-Analytics-Dashboard

Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive analysis of cryptocurrency market performance by tracking price movements, trading volume, market capitalization, and historical trends across multiple cryptocurrencies.

Business Objectives
• Monitor cryptocurrency market performance
• Analyze historical price trends
• Track market capitalization growth
• Evaluate trading volume patterns
• Compare cryptocurrency performance across different years

Dashboard Features
• High vs Low Price Analysis
• Open vs Close Price Trends
• Daily Trading Volume Analysis
• Market Capitalization Tracking
• Interactive Cryptocurrency Filters
• Dynamic Year Selection
• KPI Cards for Key Market Metrics

Tools Used
• Power BI
• Power Query
• DAX
• Data Modeling
• Data Visualization

Key Insights
• Bitcoin recorded the highest market dominance.
• Trading volume increased significantly during high-volatility periods.
• Market capitalization experienced substantial growth during bull market cycles.
• Price fluctuations varied considerably between cryptocurrencies.

Business Value
This dashboard enables investors and analysts to monitor cryptocurrency performance, identify market trends, and make data-driven investment decisions through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.
